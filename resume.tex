\section{Research Interest}
In my \href{https://rlprincipe.wixsite.com/reneprincipejr/thesis}{undergraduate research}, I performed color vision tasks such as hyperspectral reconstruction from digital RGB images using machine learning algorithms to cater art, heritage conservation, and remote sensing applications. For my master's thesis, I am currently working on using VIIRS nighttime lights data to assess the economic progress of resettlement areas and \href{https://github.com/reneprincipejr/Light-in-the-darkest-times}{ quantify electrification recovery rates in the aftermath of a disaster using the Black Marble product suite}.
